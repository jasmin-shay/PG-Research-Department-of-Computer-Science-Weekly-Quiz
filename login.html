📋 How It Works

Three steps to quiz glory

Every Monday a new challenge drops. Attempt it before Sunday midnight to stay in the running for the semester championship.

✍️
Step 01
Register & Enrol
Create your portal account once. Each week, fill in the quick registration form with your name, department, roll number, and photo before the quiz opens.
⚡
Step 02
Attempt the Quiz
Answer 10 carefully curated CS questions within the time limit. Questions cover programming, data structures, algorithms, and technology concepts.
🏆
Step 03
Earn Your Badge
Top scorers receive 🥉 Bronze, 🥈 Silver, or 🥇 Gold badges. Semester Gold champions are honoured at the annual prize ceremony with a cash award.
🏆 Hall of Fame

Weekly Champions

The top scorer from each completed week. Every champion is recognised by the PG & Research Dept of CS.

🏅 Recognition Programme

Badge Holders

Awarded by the department at the end of each semester. Gold badge holders are invited to the annual prize ceremony.

🎓 About This Portal

Organised by the
PG & Research Department of Computer Science

Quizify is the official academic quiz platform of Nilgiri College of Arts & Science, built and maintained by the PG & Research Department of Computer Science. The weekly quiz programme invites students from all departments to engage with computing, programming, and technology.

Beyond weekly quizzes, Quizify powers live classroom quiz sessions for faculty — create a quiz, share a code, and monitor your students in real time.

📅
Weekly Quizzes
A fresh CS challenge every Monday, open to all departments across the college.
🖥️
Live Classroom Quizzes
Teachers create instant quiz sessions with a 6-digit code. Students join and compete live.
📊
Real-Time Monitoring
Faculty track who joined, who left early, and scores as they come in — live.
🏅
Semester Badge Programme
Consistent performers earn Bronze, Silver, or Gold recognition. Cash prizes await Gold champions.
